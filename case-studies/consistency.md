# Consistency

## The Goal

This ecosystem shows how methods were applied using consistency. Every navigation decision — where a return link sits, whether a folder duplicates content or points to it, how a case study is structured.

This isn't a stylistic preference. It's the same principle a product applies to its own users, applied here to the portfolio: respect the reader's time.

## Why It Matters

A reader who has to relearn navigation on every page spends their attention on the structure instead of the work. Consistency is what lets that attention go entirely to the content, because the container never asks a second question.

There are always two options at any link: follow it, or skip it. Neither is penalized. Going deeper never becomes mandatory to understand what's on the surface, and skipping never means missing something the surface needed.

## Applied Methods

Consistency isn't enforced by one single rule — it's pursued through several concrete methods, applied wherever they fit the problem.

**[Layering as a Method](./the-layering-principle.md)** is the clearest of them: how depth is organized so a reader always knows how much they're being asked to hold at once.
