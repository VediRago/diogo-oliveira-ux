# Scene Blueprint

## Purpose

A scene blueprint holds only what's specific to one scene — everything that repeats belongs at the act level instead.

## Content

**Purpose** — what this scene needs to do emotionally or structurally.

**Action Flow** — the clean physical sequence, numbered.

**Dialogue / Cue Attachments** — lines, gestures, and interpretation, attached to the Action Flow numbers. Silent beats get a Cue only, never `Dialogue: None`.

**Scene Summary** — placed last, generated from the structure rather than written first and reverse-justified.

## Rule

If a global rule needs restating inside a scene to make sense, it belongs at the act level, not here. A scene blueprint should be unreadable in isolation from its act — that's the sign the division of responsibility is working, not a flaw.
